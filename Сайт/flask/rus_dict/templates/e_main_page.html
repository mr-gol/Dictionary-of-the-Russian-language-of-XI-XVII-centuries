About the project

The main point of this project is making an online version of the ongoing dictionary of Russian languge of XI–XVII centuries. This project was initialised by the dictionary's editors Ya. А. Penkova, R. N. Krivko, М. I. Chernysheva and is being worked on in cooperation with V.V. Vinogradov Russian Language Institute of the Russian Academy of Sciences.
